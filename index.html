Hello my name is Mio

Artist and designer living in Paris
One of the brightest representatives of contemporary art in Belarus and Europe, is rapidly and confidently gaining popularity in painting and fashion design, as well as in collaboration with major world's brands: Lexus,Sber Bank, Inglot cosmetics and Swiss watch Beam.
Mio's works are in large private collections in France,Cyprus,Turkey,Germany,Ukraine,Russia and Belarus in the same collections with such artists as Malevich, Kandinsky,Richter,Andy Warhol, Banksy and Crypto Punk. Cooperation with the catalog NFT GENERATION ART SHOW, Helsinki. Collaboration with the gallery ARTSI in Paris.
